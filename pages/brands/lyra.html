Lyra is one of the oldest pencil manufacturers in the world, with its legacy beginning in Germany in 1806. Now part of the F.I.L.A. Group, Lyra is globally renowned for its precision-crafted pencils and stationery.A world legend since 1806, Lyra joined the FILA family in 2008. With quality products trusted in schools and offices, Lyra has expanded into high-grade tools for artists, architects, designers, and students—always with a close eye on innovation and the environment.
