Lyra is one of the oldest pencil manufacturers in the world, originating from Germany with a legacy dating back to 1806. Now part of the F.I.L.A. Group, Lyra is recognized globally for its precision-crafted pencils and innovative stationery products.The art of creativity. LYRA, one of the oldest colouring pencil brands in the world, joined the FILA family in 2008, already a world legend since 1806, with its quality products for schools and offices. LYRA has expanded its range of top quality products for graphic arts and more, using efficient methods and with a close eye on the environment.The brand is particularly known for its high-quality color pencils, graphite pencils, and specialty art supplies for both professional and educational use. Lyra products are trusted by artists, architects, designers, and students alike.
