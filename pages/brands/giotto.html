Giotto is a highly respected brand under the F.I.L.A. Group, known for its comprehensive range of school and art supplies. Established with a focus on children and creativity, Giotto brings color into the hands of young artists with safe and reliable tools.The brand is known for its vibrant color markers, crayons, watercolors, and tempera paints that encourage free expression and artistic development from early education. Each product is designed with high safety standards, making Giotto a favorite in classrooms and homes around the world.
